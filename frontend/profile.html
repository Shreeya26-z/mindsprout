❓ Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How does XP work?

You earn +10 XP for logging your mood and +5 XP for completing habits. Every 50 XP levels you up!

How do I chat with a specialist?

Go to the Specialists page, find an available specialist and click Chat or Book.

Is my journal private?

Yes! Your journal entries are completely private and only visible to you.

How do I reset my streak?

Your streak resets if you miss a day of activity. Log a mood or complete a habit daily to keep it going!
